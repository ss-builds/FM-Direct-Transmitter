# FM Transmitter



##### Problem:

**BT FM transmitter for playing custom audio (cheap one from Amazon) leads to poor audio quality/volume loss.**



&#x09;I could replace the factory head unit with an aftermarket unit, but the good ones are incredibly expensive, look cheap, and I want to maintain the OEM look by keeping the original head unit with the car. I would try to replace the antenna (corrosion due to East coast :/), however, I can't really find a replacement without going to the junkyard, which may or may not be in the same condition or perhaps worse.

&#x09;I did find a solution that replaces the factory antenna with a connection to a BT module or something like that. However, these modules are not only expensive but also there is not much online about these solutions. ***Custom has to be the way, right?***

&#x09;*(I mean yeah, I could do custom antenna, but I like having better audio quality)*



##### Criteria:

* BT capable
* Upgraded audio quality (relative to current solution)
* Easy to control
* Able to switch from FM mode to BT mode



##### Constraints:

* Cheap as possible
* OEM+ (no replacing head-unit, no major modifications, passive)
* Interference from other signals
